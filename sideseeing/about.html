Rafael J Pezzuto Damaceno

rafael.damaceno@ime.usp.br

PhD in Computer Science from the Federal University of ABC, with expertise in the areas of Human-Computer Interaction and Scientometrics. Since the master's degree, he has been involved in studies on the accessibility of mobile devices. During his doctoral studies, he developed the Acácia Platform, a tool that maps guidance relationships in postgraduate programs in Brazil.

Currently, he is a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of São Paulo, where he works on projects in the field of Computer Vision and Deep Learning for Urban Informatics.
